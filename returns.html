Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Return Eligibility

Items may be returned within 7–14 days of delivery (as specified on the product page). Products must be unused, uninstalled, and in original packaging.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or special-order items

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number. Unauthorized returns may be refused.

4. Refunds

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after inspection.

5. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for resolution.

7. Contact

Email: returns@autofitsusa.com
